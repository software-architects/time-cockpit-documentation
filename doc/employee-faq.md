# Employee FAQ

This FAQ answers common questions for employees who use time cockpit to track work time, request absences, and review their daily entries.

> [!NOTE]
> This FAQ focuses on everyday usage for employees. For administrative questions or role-specific features, see the [User FAQ](user-faq.md) or the relevant role FAQ.

> [!NOTE]
> Reviewed in April 2026.

## Getting Started

### How do I log in to time cockpit?

Open `https://web.timecockpit.com` in your browser and sign in with the login method configured for your organization.

If you are unsure which sign-in method to use, ask your administrator.

**See Also:**
- [Web Client](getting-started/web-client.md)
- [Account Management](account-management/account-management.md)

---

### What should I do on my first day using time cockpit?

A simple first-day checklist is:

1. Log in successfully.
2. Open the **Timesheet** module.
3. Open the [Time Sheet Calendar](timesheet-calendar/calendar.md).
4. Create your first entry.
5. Confirm with your manager which projects or tasks you should use.

**See Also:**
- [Time Sheet Calendar](timesheet-calendar/calendar.md)
- [Working with Timesheet Entries](timesheet-calendar/working-with-timesheet-entries.md)

## Daily Time Tracking

### How do I quickly track my time each day?

Use the [Time Sheet Calendar](timesheet-calendar/calendar.md) and create entries as you work or at the end of the day.

In the standard calendar, you can create an entry by:

- double-clicking in the calendar
- clicking **+ Add**
- pressing `CTRL + I`

**See Also:**
- [Working with Timesheet Entries](timesheet-calendar/working-with-timesheet-entries.md)
- [Time Sheet Calendar](timesheet-calendar/calendar.md)

---

### What if I forget to log time for a day?

Open the missing day in the calendar and add the entries retroactively.

Use your calendar, notes, or other work records to reconstruct the day as accurately as possible.

**See Also:**
- [Working with Timesheet Entries](timesheet-calendar/working-with-timesheet-entries.md)
- [Time Sheet Calendar](timesheet-calendar/calendar.md)

---

### How do I track time when I work on multiple things in a day?

Create separate entries for the different work blocks of the day.

A practical standard approach is to use one entry per meaningful activity or project block and avoid overlapping entries.

**See Also:**
- [Working with Timesheet Entries](timesheet-calendar/working-with-timesheet-entries.md)
- [Time Sheet Calendar](timesheet-calendar/calendar.md)

## Vacation & Time Off

### How do I request vacation?

Use **User -> Vacation**.

You can also create a vacation request from the **Time Sheet Calendar**. This is helpful if you want to choose the dates while looking at your existing entries and visible absence times in the calendar.

Vacation can be entered as a full-day absence or as an hourly absence.

1. Create a new vacation entry.
2. Enter the dates.
3. Save the record.

If the approval workflow is enabled, the request is then reviewed by the configured approver.

**See Also:**
- [Absence Time Management](employee-time-tracking/absence-time-management.md)
- [User FAQ](user-faq.md#how-do-i-request-vacation-time)

---

### Can I cancel or change my vacation request?

Open your request in **User -> Vacation** or from the **Time Sheet Calendar** and check whether it is still editable.

You can typically change or cancel your own vacation request directly only if:

- it is your own record
- it is not approved yet, or absence approval is not required for you
- the first day of the vacation is still after your booking completion date

If you cannot change it directly, contact the approver or HR.

If the approval workflow is enabled, rejected absence requests are deleted after rejection.

**See Also:**
- [Absence Time Management](employee-time-tracking/absence-time-management.md)
- [HR Administrator FAQ](hr-administrator-faq.md)

---

### What do I do if I'm sick?

Follow your company's communication process first, then record the absence in **User -> Sick Leave**.

You can also create sick leave from the **Time Sheet Calendar**. Like vacation and compensatory time off, sick leave can be entered as a full-day absence or as an hourly absence.

As a practical standard flow:

1. Create a new sick-leave record.
2. Enter the relevant date range.
3. Add a description if your company uses it.
4. Save the record.

If the sick-leave approval workflow is enabled, the record is processed in the same absence workflow as vacation and compensatory time off.

If approval is required for your user, the sick-leave record stays pending until it is reviewed. If approval is not required for your user, new sick-leave records are approved automatically when the workflow is enabled globally.

**See Also:**

## Technical Questions

### Can I use time cockpit on my phone or tablet?

Yes. The web client runs in modern browsers and has a responsive interface for desktops, laptops, and tablets.

You can also install time cockpit as a Progressive Web App from the browser.

The standard web client requires an internet connection.

**See Also:**
- [Web Client](getting-started/web-client.md)
- [User FAQ](user-faq.md#can-i-use-time-cockpit-on-mobile-devices)

---

### What if I have technical problems or can't log in?

Check the configured login method, your browser, and your network connection first. If the problem continues, contact your internal administrator or `support@timecockpit.com`.
